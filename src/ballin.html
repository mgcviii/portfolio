BALLIN

The main objective of this project was to develop a user-friendly website with a responsive design, suitable for both desktop and mobile screens. Additionally, we were tasked with creating a unique logo for the site. Our individual interests guided the project, and we had to adhere to specific website design principles, with my chosen style being "minimalist." The website's theme revolved around basketball, and it was named "Ballin."
